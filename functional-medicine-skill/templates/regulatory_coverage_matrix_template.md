## Status Legend

- `permitted` — Authoritative source confirms authorization for this category/jurisdiction
- `conditional` — Permitted with conditions; all conditions must be documented
- `prohibited` — Explicitly not authorized or banned
- `unknown` — No authoritative assessment located; BLOCKS clearance
- `not_assessed` — Assessment not yet performed; BLOCKS finalization
