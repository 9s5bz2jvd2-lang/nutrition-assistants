## Evidence Status Legend

- `supported` — Adequate human clinical evidence from authoritative sources
- `weak` — Some evidence, low quality or limited applicability
- `absent` — No human clinical evidence found; NOT proof of safety
- `conflicting` — Contradictory evidence; must present both sides
- `animal_only` — Only animal evidence; no human efficacy claim permitted
- `traditional_only` — Only traditional/classical record; no clinical efficacy claim permitted
- `mechanistic_only` — Only mechanistic/in vitro evidence; no clinical efficacy claim permitted
