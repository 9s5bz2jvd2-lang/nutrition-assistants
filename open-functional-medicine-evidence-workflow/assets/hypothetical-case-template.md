## Reasoning exercise

1. **Observation sort** — label each item as `FACT` or `CONTEXT`.
2. **Red-flag check** — confirm no acute or life-threatening signs are present.
3. **Six-Eye check** — identify missing information across Body/Mind/Behavior/Relationship/Time/Cause.
4. **Optional bounded H0** — form one provisional, association-only hypothesis.
5. **Evidence card** — use `assets/evidence-card-template.md` to document the H0.
6. **Review output** — compile into `assets/review-output-template.md`.

## Prohibited outputs

- No diagnosis.
- No treatment or supplement recommendation.
- No dosing.
- No real patient data.
- No claim that the scenario proves any mechanism or intervention works.

## Debrief questions

- What evidence debt remains?
- What contradictions or alternative explanations exist?
- What would a clinician need to know before forming any clinical impression?
