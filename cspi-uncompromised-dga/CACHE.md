## 5. Gotchas

1. **"Official vs. uncompromised" confusion** — Users may not know there is a difference. Always clarify which version you are citing, especially for alcohol.
2. **Cup eq ≠ measured cup** — Raisins, tomato paste are concentrated; salad greens are airy. Do not treat "1 cup eq" as "1 measured cup."
3. **Oz eq ≠ measured ounce** — Nuts/peanut butter are concentrated (less than 1 oz); tofu/cooked beans are high-water (more than 1 oz).
4. **Children < 2 have separate rules** — No added sugars; different dairy guidance (whole milk at 12 months). Do not apply adult limits to children.
5. **Beans/peas/lentils dual counting** — Same food can be in Vegetables (cup eq) or Protein Foods (oz eq). Conversion: multiply cup eq by 4.
6. **Alcohol 1 drink/day is NOT the official DGA** — It is the 2020 DGAC recommendation that the Departments rejected. The official DGA still allows 2/day for men.
