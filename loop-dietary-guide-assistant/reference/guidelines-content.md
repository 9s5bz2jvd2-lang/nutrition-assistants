# Guidelines Content — Operational Summary of the 5 Uncompromised DGA Guidelines

> This is the dietary knowledge skill for the loop. It is loaded by both maker and checker sub-agents. For full details, see the parent `cspi-uncompromised-dga` skill.

## Quick reference: The 5 Guidelines

| # | Guideline | One-line rule |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Follow a healthy dietary pattern at every life stage | Age-specific rules apply; never too early or late |
| 2 | Customize and enjoy nutrient-dense choices | Cultural, personal, and economic adaptation is expected |
| 3 | Focus on food group needs with nutrient-dense foods | Quantitative targets exist; "Eat Healthy Your Way" pattern |
| 4 | Limit added sugars, sat fat, sodium, and alcohol | Specific numerical limits below |
| 5 | Prioritize sustainable healthy diets | 5 practices: plant protein ↑, red meat ↓, tap water, reduce waste, whole foods |

## Life-stage quick rules

| Stage | Key rule |
|---|---|
| 0–6 mo | Exclusive human milk (or iron-fortified formula); vitamin D supplement |
| ~6 mo | Introduce complementary foods by developmental readiness; include allergenic foods |
| 6–12 mo | Continue human milk; vary flavors/textures; iron + zinc for breastfed infants |
| < 2 yr | NO added sugars; limit high-sodium foods; whole milk at 12 mo |
| 2+ yr | Standard dietary pattern applies; fat-free/low-fat dairy |
| Pregnant/lactating | Specific sub-guidelines; avoid alcohol |
| Older adult | Emphasize nutrient density; address underconsumed nutrients |

## Quantitative limits (ages 2+)

| Component | Limit |
|---|---|
| Added sugars | < 10% of calories/day |
| Saturated fat | < 10% of calories/day (replace with unsaturated, especially plant sources) |
| Sodium | < 2,300 mg/day (< 14 yr: even less) |
| Alcohol | ≤ 1 drink/day for both sexes (uncompromised); official DGA differs |

## 2,000-calorie "Eat Healthy Your Way" pattern

| Food Group | Amount | Unit |
|---|---|---|
| Vegetables | 2¼ | cup eq/day |
| Fruits | 2 | cup eq/day |
| Grains | 6 | oz eq/day (≥3 whole, <3 refined) |
| Dairy & fortified soy alternatives | 3 | cup eq/day |
| Protein foods | 7 | oz eq/day |
| Oils | 27 | g/day |

**Protein subgroups (weekly):** Beans/peas/lentils 10 oz eq; Nuts/seeds/soy 4.5 oz eq; Seafood 8 oz eq; Meat/poultry/eggs 26 oz eq

## Underconsumed nutrients
- Vitamin D
- Calcium
- Potassium
- Dietary fiber

## Direction shifts
- Whole grains ↑, refined grains ↓
- Plant-based proteins ↑, red/processed meat ↓
- Unsaturated fats ↑, saturated fats ↓

## Alcohol: three key points (uncompromised, 2020 DGAC)
1. Do not begin or continue drinking for health reasons
2. Drinking less is generally better at all levels of consumption
3. Recommended limit: ≤ 1 drink/day for both women and men

> ⚠️ This is the uncompromised position. The official 2025–2030 DGA may differ. Always clarify which version you are citing.

## Sustainable diet: 5 practices (CSPI + Center, Guideline 5)
1. Prioritize plant-based proteins
2. Limit red and processed meat
3. Make tap water primary beverage
4. Minimize food waste
5. Choose unpackaged whole foods
