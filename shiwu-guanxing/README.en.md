# 食物观星 · Food, Nutrition & Stargazing Learning Hub

English version translated from the existing Chinese README.

A static website planned and maintained by Runyuan Wang: nutrition popular science, lifestyle health, stargazing exploration, and public original-text entry points plus in-page search for 11 National Health Commission dietary-nutrition guide directions.

## Page Content

- Home page: a public popular-science entry point for food, rhythm, and the starry sky.
- Authoritative guides: original dietary-nutrition guide entry points, seasonal menu clues, and dietary-formula search.
- Nutrition and astronomy: lightweight popular-science content for the public.
